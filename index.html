Our Story

Whew, you made it! Now, are you ready for some fun? We’ve got plenty of projects to show you…

Welcome to MeisterMade Crafts, a small business dedicated to sharing beautiful projects with the world. At MeisterMade we crochet, knit, sew, paint, glitter, glue, measure, cut, tape, and arrange; we do all kinds of activities, but we just say we’re busy “crafting”. We love making things, especially for other people. And we want to make something for you!

You can check out all of our projects in our Catalog, or you can catch a glimpse of our beautiful items in the gallery. If you’re interested in making one of these crafts your own, be sure to stop by our Etsy shop, where you can purchase any of our items. We even personalize, so don’t hesitate to contact us with special requests!

We look forward to sharing our projects with you. If it has a MeisterMade logo, you know it was made with love!

Newest Addition

What could be more “Wisconsin” than a spotted cow? The inspiration to add this animal to our inventory comes from a few different places. First off, we are based out of Wisconsin - cows are an important part of our incredible dairy tradition! Second, Wisconsin is home to Spotted Cow, a craft beer lovingly brewed by New Glarus Brewing Company. Taylor, our owner and chief-crafter, grew up only a few minutes away from New Glarus and now her dad works there!

Our hope is that people of all ages can enjoy this cow. Whether it’s a youngster who has a new pal to bring along on a road trip, or somebody who is still a youngster at heart and would like to add this to a shelf to remind them of another time – maybe even to represent their favorite brew…
